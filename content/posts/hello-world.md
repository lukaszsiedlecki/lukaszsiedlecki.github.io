Every software engineer has started with a "Hello World" program, haven't they? 
So, it only felt right that this blog should begin with the same tradition. :)

I decided to start this blog to improve my writing skills. I truly believe that writing makes us better engineers, because to write clearly is to understand deeply. It forces us to be more aware of what we know and, more importantly, what we don't.

My focus here will be on the fundamentals - the core principles that often get overlooked in today's fast-paced world. While everyone is eager to reach their goals, the understanding of *how* things actually work under the hood is sometimes lost.

I've been that person too. Despite having a solid engineering background, I realized one day that I had lost some of my curiosity. Now is the time to rebuild and refine my skills.

It isn't too late. In fact, it's just in time.

Stay in touch. There is more to come.
